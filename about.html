BIO

Panda bear Po works in his father's noodle restaurant. Soon he will take over and prepare the famous secret noodle soup, the last secret ingredient of which only his father knew until now. At night, Po dreams of fighting against the evil of his world alongside the Furious Five (Tiger, Crane, Monkey, Praying Mantis and Viper), students of the famous Kung Fu master Shifu.

6 June 2008

Kung Fu Panda

Panda Po is a fan of kung fu. However, he works in his family's pasta restaurant and is not very prone to sports. Po's dreams come true when he is unexpectedly assigned to fulfill an ancient prophecy. He has now joined the world of kung fu alongside the legendary Fearless Five. Kung fu master Shifu had the opportunity to fight side by side with Tiger, Crane, Mantis, Viper Snake and Monkey. However, when Tai Lung escapes from prison and appears before them, it will be up to Po to defend the people of Peace Valley against the approaching danger.

26 May 2011

Kung Fu Panda 2

The angry five battle a bad guy trying to destroy China and Kung Fu with an unstoppable secret weapon. Po, our hero from the first movie, appears again in the leading role. The angry five set off on a journey to China to stop this weapon. But how can our hero Po stop this weapon that will stop Kung Fu? Of course, he must search for this in his past, and only in this way will he reveal his mysterious powers, and in the meantime, a strong "friendship" emerges between Kaplan and Po.

29 January 2016

Kung Fu Panda 3

Continuing his journey to become a perfect Kung-fu master, Po begins teaching after his master Shifu retires, but this is much more difficult than he thought. Meanwhile, he meets his real father, Li. While he is having a pleasant time with Li, the evil Kai starts defeating all the kung-fu masters and stealing their powers. Po's only solution is to go to a mountain village where only pandas live and teach kung-fu to the fun-loving and clumsy pandas there.

8 March 2024

Kung Fu Panda 3

As he prepares to become the Spiritual Leader of the Valley of Peace, Po searches for his successor as the new Dragon Warrior while fighting a new enemy called "The Chameleon".
